Experimental Edition

The Experimental edition are experimental builds of CodeGlass with features that might not be released yet (or at all) to the public as they are not proven stable enough.
See the details to know which features that are.

This subscription is meant for people who want to support us fully; most features will come to the other editions within a few updates.
We might even stop offering it if we sell to many of them.
